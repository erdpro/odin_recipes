Scrambled eggs recipe

Description

Scrambled egggs is a great way to start your day or as a quick healthy snack.

The key to cooking good scrambled eggs is timing. Even half a minute too long on the pan can lead to dry eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • A splash of olive oil
  • Whatever spices you have in your pantry
  • Dash of salt
  • A splash of milk

Steps

  1. Combine all your ingredients into a bowl and whisk till the mixture is homogenous
  2. Heat up a pan to medium heat and add your mixture
  3. Gently move the mixture around with a wooden spooon until it starts to solidfy
  4. Once solidification has started, turn the heat off but keep the eggs on the pan
  5. Take the eggs off once you've reached your desired consistency
