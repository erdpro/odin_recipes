Instant noodles recipe

Description

5 minute noodles is a great way to quickly get energy into your body.

There is nothing fancy here and you probably shouldn't need this guide.

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles packet
  • Water
  • A bowl

Steps

  1. Heat up water in the stove in a small pot
  2. Add your instant noodles to the mixture
  3. Wait until the noodles are soft and remove
  4. Add any spices that came with your packet of instant noodles
