Rice recipe

Description

Rice is a common staple of diets in most households. It requires very little ingredients apart from itself and is incredibly flexible. It can be combined with an endless number of dishes.

The key to cooking good rice is timing. All you really need is a pot and some water, no need for a fancy rice cooker or special ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of white rice, like Basmati
  • A splash of olive oil
  • 25g of unsalted butter
  • Teaspoon of salt
  • (Optional) 1/2 cup of corn kernels

Steps

  1. Heat up your stainless steel pot, adding a layer of olive oil and the butter
  2. Once the butter is melted, add your rice
  3. Proceed to stir your rice in the oil and butter mixture until the rice starts to brown
  4. Add your water, generally 1.5:1 water to rice ratio and put the lid on the pot
  5. (Optional) Add your corn to the rice
  6. Bring to a boil then turn the heat down to low
  7. Once the rice has absorbed the water and you can't see any bubbles, turn the heat off and cover the lid with a kitchen towel
  8. Let the rice sit for 10 minutes before serving
